ZOHO CONSULTING

As a Zoho Consultants our job is to understand your business process and create a more efficient improved Business process which can be mapped into Zoho Products. Our team will sit with you to discuss your Sales, Operation, Support and other process involved in your business based on the discussion we will create a blueprint which will show you how your business processes can be mapped/automated using different Zoho products, how the data will flow from Zoho Product to another and which team will have access to which Zoho Product for example Sales and Operation team will have access Zoho CRM modules, Account team will have access to Zoho Books and support team will have access to Zoho Desk.

We Offer Following Zoho Consulting Service

Business Process Analysis

Our Zoho certified consultant  will conduct a discover mapping session with you and will develop a blueprint explaining how your business can be mapped/automated using Zoho Products.

Zoho Integration

Our team knows in and out of Zoho API, Pre-defined integrations with Zoho services, Workflows, Web hooks, Connections, SDKs, Web Apps so we can offer you the best advice if you project required any kind of Integration with in Zoho products or with any third party services.

Custom Portal

Zoho CRM, Zoho books, Creator offers anative client portal for customers but most of the time these native client portal does not fulfil the needs of business requirements so then it comes to development of custom portal for clients or staff. In past couple of years we have developed more than 50+ custom portal using PHP/ Zoho APIand Zoho Creator/Zoho API. So we can offer a good strategy to develop these portal either using PHP/ Zoho APIand Zoho Creator/Zoho API..
